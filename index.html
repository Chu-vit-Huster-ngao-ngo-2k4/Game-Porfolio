ABOUT ME

My name is Bui Van Binh, a Game Designer with a technical foundation from Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST). What drives my passion for game development is the intersection of logic and creativity—how different disciplines unite to build a seamless interactive experience.

With a background spanning Game Design, Level Design, Scripting, and Visual Effects, I focus on creating systems that are not only functional but emotionally engaging. Whether I am architecting complex level progressions for global puzzle titles or scripting tactical combat for award-winning jam projects, I always strive to tell a story through gameplay, ensuring every interaction feels purposeful, polished, and "smooth as butter."

Toybox Sort Challange (2025)

Level Design 3 months

For this 2D cozy matching puzzle game, I leaned heavily into my level design and scripting background to transform a simple sorting mechanic into a deeply engaging experience. It was essential to develop an item-spawning logic that felt natural yet challenging, maximizing the "spatial puzzle". When designing the 200+ levels, I aimed for a specific "satisfaction rhythm"—balancing cluttered chaos with a seamless sorting flow. With my background in VFX, adding that final layer of visual polish to the interactions made the game feel as smooth as butter!
